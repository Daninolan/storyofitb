Future of ITB

Even though we are looking into the past of itb and its story, i can't help but mention that in 2019 the college will be merging with 2 other colleges, Institute of Technology Tallaght and Dublin Institute of Technology, this will form a new Technological University for Dublin, this will be the first Technological University in Ireland and all current students will graduate with a higher degree, this is a massive achievement for the college of ITB and will only make things better as we look into the future and start of TU Dublin come January 1st 2019.
