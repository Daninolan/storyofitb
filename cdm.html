What is CDM?

CDM(creative Digital Media) is a course that has been around for a while and in it's time a lot has changed within the course from each subject within the module and sometimes even completly new subjects, but what is CDM really? CDM is a module built to make others get into the working world with many jobs like

  • Video Production
  • Video Recording
  • Sound Recording/editing
  • Web Design/devlopment
