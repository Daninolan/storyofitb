My personal Experience In CDM by Dani Nolan

So iv'e been at ITB 1 and a half years now and i'm working towards finishing my 2nd year, i have to save i'm really enjoying the course and everything it has to offer, i mostly love the projects where i can express my creative freedom like in story telling/video editing/photoshop/illustrator/webdesign and so much more, over the years iv'e made a few friends who have helped me a lot with my work and iv'e helped them, iv'e done many projects over the years and i have to say my favorite has to be the one i did in story telling with ian in symester 2 last year, i love making and telling a good story, it's part of the reason i came to ITB in the first place which was to get better and editing and start to make stories and video animations, some of the better aspects of the course is that you can work in your own time which i feel is great but i do wish sometimes the deadlines were a little longer. personaly though it's managable, some of the other things that iv'e enjoyed in CDM was how open and friendy everyone is at ITB from the lecturers to the students, a big part of me is happy i went to ITB and i am looking forward to changing into TU Dublin, before i go you can see some stuff below with a link to my cv as well as some of the projects i did over the years, thanks for taking the time to read this :)
